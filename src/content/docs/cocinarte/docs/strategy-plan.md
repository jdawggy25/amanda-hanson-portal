## Recommendations

### Critical Priority - Fix Page Speed (Weeks 1-2)

The slow page speed is your #1 barrier to SEO success. It's hurting both rankings and conversions.

#### Action 1: Optimize All Images (URGENT)

**What:** Compress and resize all photos on your website
**Why:** Images are causing 60-70% of your slow load time

**How:**
- Use tools like TinyPNG or Squoosh to compress images
- Resize images to actual display size (not larger)
- Convert images to modern formats (WebP)
- Add lazy loading for below-the-fold images

**Expected Impact:**
- Load time: 26s → 8-10s (60% improvement)
- Performance score: 55-61 → 75-80
- Ranking boost: Potential 3-5 position improvement

**Timeline:** 2-3 hours | **Cost:** Free (DIY) or $100-200

#### Action 2: Enable Caching

**What:** Configure your server to store frequently-used files
**Why:** Repeat visitors will load your site much faster

**Expected Impact:**
- Returning visitor speed: 50-70% faster
- Server load: Reduced

**Timeline:** 30-60 minutes | **Cost:** Free

#### Action 3: Review Hosting Performance

**What:** Assess if your current hosting is adequate
**Why:** Slow server response adds unnecessary delay

**Expected Impact:**
- Initial response: 1-2 seconds faster

**Timeline:** 1-2 hours | **Cost:** Potentially $10-30/month additional
