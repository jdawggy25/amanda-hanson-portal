# Local SEO & Google Business Profile Guide

Complete local SEO strategy for Casita Azul's 11+ locations to dominate Portland metro search results.

---

## Why Local SEO is Critical

For a multi-location childcare business:
- **92% of parents** choose childcare within 10 miles of home
- **46% of Google searches** have local intent
- **Google Business Profile** drives most local visibility
- **Map Pack** results get more clicks than organic for local searches
- **Multi-location strategy** = multiple chances to rank

## Google Business Profile Setup

### Creating Profiles for Each Location

Each Casita Azul location needs its own Google Business Profile:

1. **Go to**: business.google.com
2. **Click**: "Manage now" or "Add your business"
3. **Business name**: "Casita Azul Spanish Immersion Preschool - [City/Neighborhood]"
4. **Category (Primary)**: "Child Care Agency" or "Preschool"
5. **Additional categories**:
   - Day Care Center
   - Educational Institution
   - Language School

## Reviews Strategy

### Importance of Reviews

- Reviews are a top local ranking factor
- Parents heavily research childcare reviews
- 4.5+ star rating is critical for trust
- Response to reviews matters

### Review Acquisition Process

**Step 1: Identify Happy Families**
- Parents who give verbal positive feedback
- Long-term enrolled families
- Parents who refer others

**Step 2: Make It Easy**

Create location-specific review links:
```
https://g.page/r/[YOUR-PLACE-ID]/review
```

**Step 3: Review Request Email Template**

```
Subject: Would you share your Casita Azul experience?

Hi [Parent Name],

It's been wonderful having [Child Name] at Casita Azul [Location]!
We love seeing their Spanish language development.

If you have a moment, would you share your experience with a
Google review? Your feedback helps other families discover
Spanish immersion education.

[Click here to leave a review - LINK]

Thank you so much for being part of the Casita Azul family!

Warmly,
The Casita Azul [Location] Team
```

**Step 4: In-Person Ask**

Train teachers to identify opportunities:
"We're so glad [Child] is thriving! If you get a chance, we'd love
if you could share your experience on Google. Here's a card with
the link."

### Responding to Reviews

**Positive Review Response Template:**
```
Thank you so much, [Name]! We love having [Child's name, if mentioned]
at Casita Azul [Location]. It's wonderful to hear about their Spanish
progress! Our team is grateful for families like yours. - The Casita
Azul Team
```

**Negative Review Response Template:**
```
Hi [Name], thank you for sharing your feedback. We take all parent
concerns seriously and want every family to have a positive experience.
Please contact our director at [email] so we can address your concerns
directly. We value your input and hope to make things right.
```
