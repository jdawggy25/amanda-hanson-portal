| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |

| Expert quote delays | Medium | Start outreach early, have backup quotes |
| Clinical review bottleneck | High | Schedule reviews in advance |
| Design resource constraints | Medium | Prioritize most important visuals |

- Internal linking complete
- Performance tracking established
- Initial rankings captured for baseline
