## Content Guidelines

### Each Blog Post Should Include:

1. **SEO Elements**
   - Keyword in H1, first paragraph, and throughout naturally
   - Meta title (50-60 characters)
   - Meta description (150-160 characters)
   - Alt text on all images with keywords

2. **Structure**
   - Clear H2 and H3 headings
   - Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences)
   - Bullet points and numbered lists
   - Table of contents for longer posts

3. **Internal Links**
   - Link to relevant service pages
   - Link to related blog posts
   - Include CTA to schedule tour

4. **External Credibility**
   - Cite research and studies
   - Link to authoritative sources
   - Include expert quotes when relevant

5. **Author Box**
   - Include author bio
   - Mention Casita Azul credentials
   - Photo of author (builds trust)

6. **Call to Action**
   - End with CTA section
   - Link to contact/tour page
   - Include location options
