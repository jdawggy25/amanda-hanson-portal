### YouTube Ad Creative Guidelines

**VSL Structure (5-15 min):**
1. **Hook (0-30 sec):** Pattern interrupt, bold claim
2. **Problem (30s-2min):** Agitate pain points
3. **Story (2-5min):** Personal journey, relatability
4. **Solution (5-8min):** Framework, proof
5. **Offer (8-12min):** What they get
6. **CTA (12-15min):** Clear next step

**Script Framework:**

```
HOOK: "What if everything you've been told about midlife is wrong?"

PROBLEM: "You've achieved success by every external measure—
the career, the family, the house. But there's this nagging feeling
that something's missing..."

STORY: "I know because I was there. With my PhD, my successful
practice, my picture-perfect life... I was secretly miserable..."

SOLUTION: "That's when I discovered the 5-stage Reinvention
Framework that changed everything..."

OFFER: "I've put together a free 5-day challenge that walks you
through the exact process..."

CTA: "Click the link below to join over 10,000 women who've
already started their reinvention journey."
```

### Ad Formats by Funnel Stage

| Stage | Format | Objective |
|-------|--------|-----------|
| TOFU | Video (Reels) | Awareness, video views |
| TOFU | Carousel | Traffic, engagement |
| MOFU | Video (testimonial) | Leads |
| MOFU | Image + copy | Leads |
| BOFU | Dynamic creative | Conversions |
| BOFU | Collection | Conversions |

### Creative Best Practices

**Video Ads:**
- 4:5 or 9:16 aspect ratio
- Hook in first 3 seconds
- Captions always on
- 15-30 seconds optimal
- UGC-style performs best

**Image Ads:**
- Minimal text (< 20%)
- High contrast
- Clear CTA
- Real photos > stock
- Test faces vs. no faces

**Copy Framework:**

```
HOOK: [Pattern interrupt or bold statement]

PROBLEM: [1-2 sentences on pain point]

AGITATE: [Make it worse - what happens if they don't solve]

SOLUTION: [Brief intro to your offer]

PROOF: [Social proof, stat, or testimonial]

CTA: [Clear action with benefit]
```

**Example Ad Copy:**

```
At 47, I had the career, the house, the family...
and I was secretly miserable.

Everyone told me I "had it all" but something felt
desperately wrong.

Then I discovered the 5 shifts that transformed
everything (without blowing up my life).

10,000+ women have already learned these secrets
in my free 5-Day Reinvention Challenge.

Want in? Comment "READY" and I'll send you the link.
```

**Pin Design:**
- 2:3 ratio (1000x1500)
- Text overlay: 4-6 words
- High contrast colors
- Clear, readable fonts
- Brand colors consistent
- Logo/URL on every pin

**Pin Copy:**
- Title: 40-100 characters (keyword-rich)
- Description: 100-500 characters
- Include keywords naturally
- Clear CTA
