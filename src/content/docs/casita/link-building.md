# Link Building Strategy Guide

Comprehensive link building strategy for Casita Azul to build domain authority and local relevance.

---

## Why Link Building Matters

Backlinks remain one of Google's top 3 ranking factors. For local businesses like Casita Azul:

1. **Domain Authority** - Quality links increase overall site authority
2. **Local Relevance** - Local links signal geographic relevance
3. **Referral Traffic** - Links from parenting sites = qualified visitors
4. **Trust Signals** - Being mentioned by credible sources builds trust

Casita Azul

## Anchor Text Strategy

### Natural Anchor Distribution

| Anchor Type | Percentage | Examples |
|-------------|------------|----------|
| Brand | 40% | "Casita Azul", "Casita Azul Preschool" |
| URL/Naked | 15% | "casitaazul.com" |
| Generic | 20% | "click here", "learn more", "this preschool" |
| Keyword + Brand | 15% | "Casita Azul Spanish preschool" |
| Exact Match Keyword | 10% | "Spanish immersion Portland" |

**Avoid over-optimization!** Too many exact-match anchors = Google penalty risk.

## Link Outreach Process

### Step 1: Build Prospect List

1. Use search operators to find opportunities
2. Export competitor backlinks from Ahrefs
3. Search for resource pages
4. Identify local blogs and news sites

### Step 2: Find Contact Information

- Check website for email
- Use Hunter.io
- LinkedIn for journalist contacts
- Twitter for bloggers

### Step 3: Personalize Outreach

- Reference specific content on their site
- Explain why you're a good fit
- Offer value (not just asking)
- Keep emails short (under 150 words)

### Step 4: Follow Up

- Wait 5-7 days before following up
- Maximum 2 follow-ups
- Try different subject lines
- Don't be pushy

### Step 5: Track Results

## What NOT to Do

1. **Don't buy links** - Google penalty risk
2. **Don't use PBNs** - Private blog networks are spam
3. **Don't over-optimize anchors** - Looks manipulative
4. **Don't exchange links** - "I link you, you link me" schemes
5. **Don't spam blog comments** - Waste of time, bad look
6. **Don't use automated tools for mass outreach** - Damages reputation
