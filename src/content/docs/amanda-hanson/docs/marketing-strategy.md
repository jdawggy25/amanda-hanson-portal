# Marketing Strategy Overview

## Strategic Framework

Amanda Hanson's marketing strategy leverages her existing 947K Instagram following while expanding to underutilized high-ROI channels. The approach balances organic content marketing with paid acquisition through optimized funnels.

### Marketing Goals

| Timeframe | Goal | Target |
|-----------|------|--------|
| 30 Days | Launch new site | 95+ Lighthouse score |
| 90 Days | Organic growth | 2,500 monthly visitors |
| 6 Months | Paid acquisition | 50+ clients from ads |
| 12 Months | Total revenue | $1.15M-$1.8M |

### Channel-Specific Strategies

#### 1. Instagram (Primary - Organic)

**Current Asset:** 947K followers

**Strategy:**
- Increase posting to 5-7 posts/week
- 4-5 Reels per week (algorithm favored)
- 2-3 Carousels per week (educational)
- Daily Stories (5-10 per day)
- Weekly Lives for community building

**Content Pillars:**
- Educational (40%) - Tips, frameworks, how-tos
- Inspirational (30%) - Transformations, stories
- Community (20%) - Client features, Q&As
- Promotional (10%) - Offers, events

**Conversion Path:**
```
Reel/Post → Profile Visit → Link in Bio → Lead Magnet → Email Sequence → Application
```

#### 2. Email Marketing (Primary - Owned)

**Platform:** Kit (ConvertKit)

**List Goals:**
| Timeframe | Subscribers |
|-----------|-------------|
| Current | Unknown |
| 30 Days | +500 |
| 90 Days | 5,000 |
| 6 Months | 15,000 |
| 12 Months | 30,000 |

**Key Sequences:**
1. Welcome Sequence (7 emails, 14 days)
2. Nurture Sequence (weekly value)
3. Launch Sequence (14 days)
4. Abandoned Cart (4 emails)
5. Re-engagement (3 emails)

**Metrics Targets:**
| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Open Rate | 25-35% |
| Click Rate | 2.5-3.5% |
| Unsubscribe Rate | <0.5% |

#### 3. YouTube Ads (Primary - Paid)

**Why YouTube:**
- 8-12x lower CPL than Facebook
- 45% of target demo uses weekly
- Educational mindset = higher intent
- Longer engagement (11.5 min avg)

**Ad Formats:**
| Format | Length | Purpose | CPL |
|--------|--------|---------|-----|
| In-Stream Skippable | 5-15 min | VSL delivery | $8-25 |
| Discovery | N/A | Capture searchers | $12-28 |
| Bumper | 6 sec | Retargeting | $11 CPM |

**Targeting:**
- Channel affinity (Mel Robbins, Marie Forleo, etc.)
- Keywords ("career change at 40", "midlife reinvention")
- Custom intent audiences
- Demographics (40-60, female, high income)

**Budget:** 35% of ad spend ($61,250/year)

#### 4. Meta Ads (Facebook/Instagram - Paid)

**Strategy:**
- Leverage Instagram following with paid amplification
- Heavy retargeting of engaged users
- Lookalike audiences from buyers

**Campaign Structure:**
```
TOFU (30% budget): Cold traffic acquisition
├── Interest-based targeting
├── Lookalike 1-3%
└── Broad with creative testing

MOFU (30% budget): Engagement retargeting
├── Video viewers 25-75%
├── Website visitors 30 days
└── Email list

BOFU (40% budget): Conversion campaigns
├── Video viewers 75%+
├── Page visitors 7 days
├── Cart abandoners
└── Application starters
```

**Budget:** 40% of ad spend ($70,000/year)

#### 5. Pinterest (Secondary - Paid + Organic)

**Why Pinterest:**
- 50% of users are 40+
- 77% are women
- 85% use for planning/decisions
- Underutilized by competitors

**Content Strategy:**
- 5-15 pins per day (scheduled)
- 10-15 themed boards
- Every pin links to website
- Repurpose blog posts → pins

**Pin Types:**
| Type | Purpose | CTA |
|------|---------|-----|
| Quote Graphics | Awareness | Profile/Follow |
| Blog Pins | Traffic | Read More |
| Lead Magnet Pins | Capture | Download |
| Program Pins | Conversion | Learn More |

**Budget:** 20% of ad spend ($35,100/year)

#### 6. TikTok (Testing)

**Why Test TikTok:**
- Fastest growing 40+ demographic (+34% YoY)
- 23% of users are 40+
- Organic reach still possible
- Authentic content performs well

**Content Strategy:**
- Repurpose Instagram Reels
- Create 2-3 TikTok-native pieces weekly
- Focus on: Relatable struggles, myth-busting, quick tips

**Budget:** 5% of ad spend ($8,775/year)

## Funnel Architecture

### Primary Funnel: 5-Day Challenge

```
Traffic Source (Ads/Organic)
        ↓
Challenge Registration Page
        ↓
Day 1-4: Value Delivery (Email + Video)
        ↓
Day 5: Live Pitch Event
        ↓
Application Page
        ↓
Discovery Call Booking
        ↓
Sales Call
        ↓
Enrollment
```

**Conversion Benchmarks:**
| Stage | Rate |
|-------|------|
| Ad → Registration | 30-50% |
| Registration → Completion | 45-60% |
| Completion → Application | 15-28% |
| Application → Call Booked | 75-90% |
| Call Show Rate | 55-70% |
| Call → Close | 35-50% |
| **Overall** | **2.9-9.8%** |

### Secondary Funnel: Webinar

```
Traffic Source
        ↓
Webinar Registration
        ↓
Reminder Sequence (5-7 emails)
        ↓
Live/Automated Webinar (60-90 min)
        ↓
Application/Checkout
        ↓
Sales Call (for high-ticket)
        ↓
Enrollment
```

**Conversion Benchmarks:**
| Stage | Rate |
|-------|------|
| Ad → Registration | 25-40% |
| Registration → Attendance | 45-60% |
| Attendance → Application | 15-25% |
| **Overall** | **1.6-5.9%** |

### Tertiary Funnel: Book

```
Traffic Source
        ↓
Book Landing Page (Free + Shipping)
        ↓
Order Bump (Audiobook, Workbook)
        ↓
Thank You Page OTO
        ↓
Email Nurture (30-60 days)
        ↓
Webinar Invitation
        ↓
Application
        ↓
Sales Call
```

**Conversion Benchmarks:**
| Stage | Rate |
|-------|------|
| Ad → Book Order | 5-15% |
| Book Order → OTO | 5-15% |
| Book → Application | 5-12% |
| **Overall** | **0.5-2.3%** |

## Retargeting Strategy

### Audience Tiers

**Tier 1 - Highest Intent (15% of budget):**
- Application starters (not completed)
- Call no-shows
- Webinar attendees (didn't buy)

**Tier 2 - Warm (20% of budget):**
- Video viewers 75%+
- Website visitors 3+ minutes
- Email clickers

**Tier 3 - Engaged (10% of budget):**
- Video viewers 25-75%
- Website visitors (any)
- Social engagers

### Cross-Platform Retargeting

```
YouTube viewer → Meta retargeting ad
Pinterest visitor → Meta retargeting
Email opener → YouTube retargeting
Website visitor → All platforms
```
