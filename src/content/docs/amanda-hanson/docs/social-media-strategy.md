### Instagram Reels Strategy

**Optimal Reel Specs:**
- Length: 15-30 seconds (sweet spot)
- Format: 9:16 vertical
- Hook in first 1-3 seconds
- Captions required (85% watch without sound)
- Trending audio when appropriate

**High-Performing Reel Formats:**
1. **Talking Head**: Direct camera, personal message
2. **Text Overlay**: Quick tips with music
3. **Trending Audio**: Relatable content with viral sounds
4. **Before/After**: Transformation showcases
5. **Day in My Life**: Behind-the-scenes relatability

### Instagram Story Strategy

**Daily Story Framework:**
```
Morning: Personal/relatable content
Midday: Value/education
Afternoon: Engagement (polls, questions)
Evening: Call-to-action or inspiration
```

**Story Features to Use:**
- Polls (2-3x per week)
- Questions (1x per week)
- Quizzes (1x per week)
- Countdown (for events/launches)
- Link stickers (every value post)
- Close Friends (for VIP content)

## Engagement Strategy

### Daily Engagement Time (30-60 min)

**Instagram (20 min):**
- Respond to all comments
- Reply to DMs
- Engage with 10-15 accounts in niche
- Story engagement (polls, questions)

**Other Platforms (10-20 min each):**
- Pinterest: Repin, comment on niche content
- YouTube: Reply to comments
- Facebook Group: Respond to posts
- LinkedIn: Comment on relevant posts

### Community Building Tactics

1. **Acknowledge every comment** on posts
2. **DM welcome** new engaged followers
3. **Feature community members** regularly
4. **Create shareable content** (quotes, tips)
5. **Host regular live sessions**
6. **Celebrate wins publicly**
