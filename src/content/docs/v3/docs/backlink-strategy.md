## Digital PR Strategy

### HARO / Journalist Outreach

**Platform**: HARO (Help a Reporter Out), Qwoted, Featured

**Target Topics**:
- Healthcare technology trends
- Wound care industry insights
- Medical software innovation
- Healthcare compliance
- Digital health transformation

**Response Protocol**:
1. Monitor queries 3x daily (morning, noon, evening)
2. Respond within 2 hours of relevant query
3. Include expert credentials
4. Provide quotable, specific insights
5. Follow up if selected

**Expert Spokespeople**:
- [CEO/Founder] - Business/industry trends
- [Clinical Advisor] - Wound care expertise
- [CTO] - Technology innovation
- [Compliance Officer] - Regulatory topics

---

### Press Release Distribution

**Newsworthy Events**:

**SEO Optimization for PRs**:
- Include target keywords naturally
- Link to relevant landing pages
- Use anchor text strategically
- Include multimedia (images, video)

## Link Quality Guidelines

### Links to Pursue

- DA 30+ domains
- Relevant to healthcare/technology
- Editorial (not paid/sponsored indicators)
- From content that receives traffic
- Natural anchor text opportunities

### Links to Avoid

- PBNs (Private Blog Networks)
- Link farms
- Irrelevant directories
- Paid links without nofollow
- Comment spam
- Forum signature links
