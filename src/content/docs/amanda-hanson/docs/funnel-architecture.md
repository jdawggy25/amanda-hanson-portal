# Funnel Architecture

## Funnel Strategy Overview

Amanda Hanson's funnel architecture is designed to convert her 947K Instagram following and paid traffic into high-ticket coaching clients. The multi-funnel approach addresses different buyer awareness levels and preferences.

### Funnel Performance Hierarchy

| Funnel Type | Conversion Rate | Best For | Complexity |
|-------------|-----------------|----------|------------|
| Challenge Funnel | 2.9-9.8% | Cold traffic, list building | High |
| Webinar Funnel | 1.6-5.9% | Warm traffic, education | Medium |
| VSL Funnel | 1.2-4.5% | Retargeting, direct response | Medium |
| Book Funnel | 0.5-2.3% | Authority building, long-term | Low |
| Application Funnel | 3-8% | Hot traffic, high intent | Low |

### Challenge Conversion Benchmarks

| Stage | Target Rate | Optimized Rate |
|-------|-------------|----------------|
| Ad → Registration | 30-50% | 45-60% |
| Registration → Day 1 Show | 70-85% | 80-90% |

### Challenge Content Framework

**Day 1: Awareness & Hope**
- Topic: "The Midlife Myth: Why Everything You've Been Told Is Wrong"
- Goal: Shift perspective, create buy-in
- Deliverable: Self-assessment worksheet
- CTA: Complete assessment, share in community

**Day 2: Identity & Vision**
- Topic: "Discovering Who You Really Are (Beyond Mom, Wife, Employee)"
- Goal: Connect with authentic desires
- Deliverable: Vision mapping exercise
- CTA: Share one insight in community

**Day 3: Obstacles & Breakthrough**
- Topic: "The 5 Hidden Blocks Keeping You Stuck"
- Goal: Identify personal obstacles
- Deliverable: Block identification worksheet
- CTA: Identify your #1 block

**Day 4: Strategy & Action**
- Topic: "Your Reinvention Roadmap"
- Goal: Provide clear path forward
- Deliverable: 90-day action plan template
- CTA: Complete first 3 action items

**Day 5: Transformation & Invitation**
- Topic: "Your Next Chapter Starts Now" (Live Event)
- Goal: Inspire action, present offer
- Deliverable: Q's Reign program overview
- CTA: Apply for program

### Webinar Structure (90 Minutes)

| Segment | Duration | Purpose |
|---------|----------|---------|
| Opening Hook | 5 min | Capture attention, promise value |
| Story/Credibility | 10 min | Build rapport, establish authority |

| Content Block 3 | 15 min | Third major teaching point |

### Application Questions Framework

**Qualifying Questions:**
1. What's your biggest challenge right now in this chapter of life?
2. What have you already tried to solve this?

5. Are you in a position to invest in yourself financially?

│  │  Hero   │  Trust indicators:             │

### Conversion Elements Checklist

**Trust Builders:**
- [ ] Testimonials with photos and full names
- [ ] Video testimonials (highest impact)
- [ ] Case study results with specifics
- [ ] Media mentions and logos
- [ ] Credential badges (PhD)
- [ ] Social proof numbers

**Urgency/Scarcity:**
- [ ] Countdown timer (if legitimate deadline)
- [ ] Limited spots messaging
- [ ] Early bird pricing
- [ ] Bonus expiration

**Risk Reversal:**
- [ ] Money-back guarantee
- [ ] Satisfaction guarantee
- [ ] "See results or..." promise

## Retargeting Strategy by Funnel Stage

### Audience Tiers

**Tier 1: Highest Intent (50% of retargeting budget)**
- Application starters (didn't complete)
- Call no-shows
- Webinar attendees (full pitch, didn't buy)
- Challenge completers (didn't apply)

**Tier 2: Warm (30% of retargeting budget)**
- Video viewers 75%+
- Page visitors 3+ minutes
- Email clickers (past 30 days)
- Partial challenge completion

**Tier 3: Engaged (20% of retargeting budget)**
- Video viewers 25-75%
- Website visitors (any)
- Social engagers
- Email openers

### Retargeting Ad Messaging

| Stage | Message Type | Example |
|-------|--------------|---------|
| Video Viewer | Curiosity/Value | "Did you see this? 3 more secrets..." |
| Page Visitor | Social Proof | "See why 10,000+ women chose..." |
| Challenge Dropout | FOMO | "You missed Day 4 - here's what happened" |
| App Abandoner | Direct | "Your application is waiting - 2 spots left" |
| Call No-Show | Personal | "I held your spot - can we reschedule?" |

## Funnel Optimization Framework

### Weekly Optimization Checklist

- [ ] Review funnel conversion rates
- [ ] Identify biggest drop-off point
- [ ] A/B test one element
- [ ] Review ad creative performance
- [ ] Check email deliverability
- [ ] Monitor page load speed

### A/B Testing Priority

| Priority | Element | Test Type |
|----------|---------|-----------|
| 1 | Headlines | Copy |
| 2 | CTA buttons | Copy + Color |
| 3 | Form length | UX |
| 4 | Social proof | Placement |
| 5 | Video vs. text | Format |
| 6 | Pricing display | Presentation |
