# Epic 06: Video & Multimedia Strategy

## Epic Overview

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Epic ID** | EPIC-06 |
| **Title** | Video & Multimedia Strategy |
| **Priority** | P2 - Medium |
| **Sprint** | Sprint 3-6 (Week 5-12) |
| **Owner** | Marketing Team |
| **Estimated Effort** | 35 story points |

## Business Value

75% of target keywords show video previews in SERPs. Video content improves engagement, builds trust, and captures video search traffic. Establishes V3 presence on YouTube for long-term organic discovery.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] YouTube channel created and optimized
- [ ] 3+ product demo videos published
- [ ] 2+ educational videos published
- [ ] 1+ testimonial video published
- [ ] Video schema implemented on site
- [ ] Videos embedded in relevant pages
