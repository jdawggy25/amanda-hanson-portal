# SEO Strategy Plan - Q1 2026

## Overall Strategy

BNC Builders' SEO strategy focuses on dominating local search results for home remodeling services in San Diego County. The primary approach is improving existing keyword rankings, creating optimized local content, and building authority through reviews and local citations.

### Primary Goals
1. Rank top 5 for primary service + city keywords
2. Build organic traffic to 500+ monthly visitors
3. Establish BNC Builders as the premier remodeling contractor in North San Diego County
