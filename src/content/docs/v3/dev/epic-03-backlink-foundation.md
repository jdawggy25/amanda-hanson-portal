# Epic 03: Backlink & Authority Foundation

## Epic Overview

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Epic ID** | EPIC-03 |
| **Title** | Backlink & Authority Foundation |
| **Priority** | P0 - Critical |
| **Sprint** | Sprint 1-2 (Week 1-4) |
| **Owner** | SEO/Marketing Team |
| **Estimated Effort** | 30 story points |

## Business Value

Build domain authority through strategic directory submissions and initial link building. Directory backlinks provide foundational authority signals and drive referral traffic from relevant audiences.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Tier 1 healthcare directories submitted (5)
- [ ] Tier 2 SaaS directories submitted (5)
- [ ] Tier 3 general tech directories submitted (5)
- [ ] All listings consistent in NAP and messaging
- [ ] Review generation campaign initiated (G2, Capterra)
- [ ] HARO monitoring established
- [ ] 15+ new referring domains acquired

## Dependencies

- Company profile assets (logo, screenshots, descriptions)
- Customer contact list for review requests
- Expert spokesperson availability for HARO
- Some directories may require payment

## Risks

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|------------|
| Directory approval delays | Medium | Submit early, follow up proactively |
| Low review response rate | Medium | Personalize outreach, offer incentives |
| HARO competition | Low | Respond quickly, be specific |

## Definition of Done

- All Tier 1-3 directories submitted
- 15+ new referring domains acquired
- HARO monitoring active
- Review campaign initiated with first reviews
- All submissions tracked and documented
