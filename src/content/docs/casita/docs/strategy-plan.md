# SEO Strategy Plan - Q1 2026

## Overall Strategy

Casita Azul's SEO strategy focuses on establishing local search visibility for Spanish immersion preschool services in the Portland metro area. The primary approach is fixing technical SEO issues, creating optimized service/location pages, and building local authority through targeted link building.

### Primary Goals
1. Rank for local Spanish preschool and daycare keywords
2. Optimize Google Business Profile for all locations
3. Build organic traffic to 300+ monthly visitors within 6 months
